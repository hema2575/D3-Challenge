Correlation Between Health Risks and Age, Income

From the D3 dabbler, there appears to be a positive relationship between Income and healthcare. As the Income distribution increases, the healthcare in the income group decreases, as observed in the case of NH & GA. But there variation in this behavior, as in WA & WI, we see that the poverty levels are same with different levels of healthcare access. The lack of healthcare is highest in TX even though NC reports the same poverty level. This could be due to factors like difference in the cost of healthcare throughout US.
